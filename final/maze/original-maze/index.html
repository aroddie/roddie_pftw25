Maze 1.0

This was my original attempt at the maze before discovering the p5.play library.

I was unsatisfied with the collision handling of the maze walls because the dot had a tendency to get stuck and phase through the wall.

This version is "playable" in that the character can be moved, but it is clearly unfinished (only the right wall has collision programmed). I wanted to keep it in as a documentation of my progress.
